Our story

Splitano began with one uncomfortable truth about bills.

People do not always need more debt. Sometimes they need a fair way to keep essential services current without feeling judged, rushed, or confused.

Founding idea

Bill relief should feel respectful, not risky.

Splitano is designed for the awkward week when an essential bill arrives before the paycheck. The goal is not limitless spending. The goal is to help households stay steady when timing gets tight.

That is why the brand centers plain language, visible repayment dates, and a tone that treats bill pressure as normal life, not a personal failure.

Mission

Make essential bill relief structured, responsible, and understandable.

Milestones

The path from bill pressure to calmer choices.

Milestone 01

Bill-pressure research

Mapped common bill categories, due-date friction, and customer support pain points so Splitano could focus on real household pressure.

Milestone 02

A bill-first promise

Kept the product centered on essential providers, giving customers a path that supports bills they already intend to pay.

Milestone 03

A calmer public experience

Shaped the website, support paths, and business story around trust, clarity, and dignity.
