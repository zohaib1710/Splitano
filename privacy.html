Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Splitano handles data collection, use, sharing, retention, and customer privacy choices.

We may collect contact details, bill information, identity details, repayment activity, device data, and messages you send us.

How We Use Information

Information may be used to assess eligibility, pay billers, manage repayments, provide support, improve products, and meet legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We may share limited data with payment processors, billers, service providers, compliance partners, and authorities where legally required.
